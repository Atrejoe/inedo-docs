Invoke Operation

Invokes a specified operation by its script alias.

Script usage:

Invoke-Operation(
	Name: <text>,
	[Arguments: <%(key1: value1, ...)>]
);

This operation may be prefixed with Core::, although this is a built-in namespace and isn't really necessary.
